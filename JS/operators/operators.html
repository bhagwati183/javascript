Operators

Operators are symbols which used to perform operations between two operands.
Example:- var Sum=10+20;
10 n 20 are operands and (+)symbol is addition operator.

Following are the operators of js:-

  1. Arithmetic Operators
  2. Assignment Operators
  3. Comparison Operators
  4. Logical Operators
  5. Special Operators

1.Arithmetic Operators:- Arithmetic operator performs mathematical operations between two operands.

+ ,- ,* ,/ ,% ,++ ,-- are arithmetic operators.

2.Assignment Operators:- Assignment operators, assign values to variables with less key strokes.
=,+=,-=,*=,/=,%= are assignment operators.

3.comparison Operators:- Comparison Operator compares two operands and returns boolean values true or false.

==,===,!=,>,<,>=,<= are comparison operators.

4.Logical Operators:- Logical operators are used to combine two or more conditions.
&&,||,! are logical operators.

5.Special Operators:- (?:)ternary operator,(,)comma operator,typeof,delete,new,instanceof,void,yield,in are special operator.
